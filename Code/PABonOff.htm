SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_36 The Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp is controlled via hardwired in directly. Meanwhile, AECU will send out Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp status by CAN signal “Passenger Airbag Indication Request”.
Below is the information of this CAN signal:
CAN signal: FrtPsngAirbagIndReqHSC1;
CAN ID: 0x163
Value:
0x0: Passenger Airbag NO indication;
0x1: Passenger Airbag ON indication;
0x2: Passenger Airbag OFF indication;
0x3: Passenger Airbag ON and OFF indication.

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_27 4.2.1 KL.15 (Hardwire) Status is HIGH
 Not a Requirement Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_48 Scope:
This section defines the Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp behavior when KL.15 (Hardwire) status is high.
 Not a Requirement Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_49 When AECU is in below condition:
1.If the status of KL.15 (hardwire) is switch to HIGH from LOW;
Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp Behavior:
1. Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp shall be ON for time threshold T1 seconds;
2. And the value of signal "FrtPsngAirbagIndReqHSC1" should be "3" during T1.

Note: In this situation, Passenger Airbag ON Lamp and Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be ON at the same time.
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_50 When AECU is in below condition:
1.If the status of KL.15 (hardwire) is HIGH;
2. And T1 is time out.
Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp Behavior:
1. Turn OFF Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp for time threshold T2 second.
2. And the value of signal "FrtPsngAirbagIndReqHSC1" should be "0" during T2.
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_51 Below is the definition of Timer T1 and T2:
1. Time threshold T1 equal to 6 seconds;
2. T2 equal to 1 second;
3. T1 shall start running after KL15=high;
4. T2 shall start running after T1 is time out;
5. Both of these two timers shall be reset to 0 when KL15=low;
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_52 Note 1:
During T1 seconds when KL.15 (Hardwire) is high, PADS status (enabled or disabled), PABL faults, PADS fault and PAB faults will not impact Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp Behavior.
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_53 Note 2:
During T2 seconds when KL.15 (Hardwire) is high, PADS status (enabled or disabled), PABL faults, PADS fault and PAB faults will not impact Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp Behavior.
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_54 When AECU is in below conditions:
1.If KL.15 (Hardwire) is HIGH;
2. And T2 is time out;
3. And system voltage is out of the range of 9~16V.
Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp Behavior:
1. Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be OFF.
2. And the value of signal "FrtPsngAirbagIndReqHSC1" should be "0".
3. Timer T3 reset.
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_55 When AECU is in below conditions:
1. KL.15 (Hardwire) is HIGH;
2. And T2 is time out;
3. And system voltage is in the range of 9~16V;
4. And Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp has fault;
Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp Behavior:
1. Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be OFF;
2. And the value of signal "FrtPsngAirbagIndReqHSC1" should be "0".
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_56 When AECU is in below conditions:
1. KL.15 (Hardwire) is HIGH
2. And T2 is time out;
3. And system voltage is in the range of 9~16V;
4. And Passenger Airbag ON Lamp has no faults;
5. And PADS is disabled;
Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp Behavior:
1. Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be ON;
2. And the value of signal "FrtPsngAirbagIndReqHSC1" should be "2".
3. Timer T3 reset.

Note: PADS is disabled means PAB is disabled, PADS is ON;
PADS is enabled means PAB is enabled, PADS is OFF;
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_57 When AECU is in below conditions:
1. KL.15 (Hardwire) is HIGH;
2. And T2 is time out;
3. And system voltage is in the range of 9 to 16 voltage;
4. And PABL has no faults;
5. And PADS is enabled;
6. And PADS/PAB has no faults.
7. And T3 is 0;
Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp Behavior:
1. Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be OFF for time threshold T3 seconds.
2. And the value of signal "FrtPsngAirbagIndReqHSC1" should be "1" during T3.
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_58 Below is the definition of Timer T3:
1. T3 should be start at the meantime when T2 is time out;
2. T3 is equal to 62 seconds.
3. T3 should be reset when KL.15 (Hardwire) is LOW or PADS is disabled, or PADS is undertemined/fault, or PAB fault or Passenger Airbag ON Lamp fault, or system voltage is out of range of 9~16V.

Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_59 When AECU is in below conditions:
1. KL.15 (Hardwire) is HIGH;
2. And T2 is time out;
3. And system voltage is in the range of 9 to 16 voltage;
4. And PABL has no faults;
5. And PADS is enabled;
6. And PADS/PAB has no faults;
7. And T3 is time out;
Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp Behavior:
1. Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be OFF;
2. And the value of signal "FrtPsngAirbagIndReqHSC1" should be "0".
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_60 When AECU is in below conditions:
1. KL.15 (Hardwire) is HIGH;
2. And T2 is time out;
3. And system voltage is in the range of 9 to 16 voltage;
4. And PABL has no faults;
5. And PADS is undetermined or PADS/PAB fault;
Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp Behavior:
1. Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be ON;
2. And the value of signal "FrtPsngAirbagIndReqHSC1" should be "3".
3. Timer T3 reset.
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_61 When AECU is in below conditions:
1. KL.15 (Hardwire) is HIGH;
2. PADS is not configured;
Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp Behavior:
1. Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be OFF;
2. And the value of signal "FrtPsngAirbagIndReqHSC1" should be "0".
Note: If PADS is not configured, PABL should also be not configured. PABL function should also be disabled.
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_79 When AECU is in below conditions:
1. KL.15 (Hardwire) is HIGH;
2. And T2 is time out;
3. And Front Algorithm is disabled or inhibited;
Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp Behavior:
1. Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be ON;
2. And the value of signal "FrtPsngAirbagIndReqHSC1" should be "2".
3. Timer T3 reset.

Note 1:After T2 is time out, the status of Passenger Airbag OFF lamp shall be continuously commanded ON when the Front Algorithm is inhibited / disabled due to any reason (except PADS status is undetermined).
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_81 “Passenger Airbag Indication Request” shall be set to default value “0” and sent to CAN message when PADS is not configured, PABL status in DID 0xB001 and EDR shall be record as “signal not available” at this special case.
Note: when PADS and PABL are configured by hardware, Passenger Airbag OFF/ON Indication in DID 0xB001 and EDR shall keep pace with PAB ON/OFF lamp HW status; when PADS configured and PABL not configured by Hardware, DID 0xB001 and EDR shall keep pace with PAB ON/OFF CAN status;
If PABL just configured by CAN, there is no “Fault” state and no need feedback “Fault” state to DID 0xB001 and EDR, just needs feedback “On”/ “Off” state to CAN message.
Note: For this requirement, Regardless of KL.15 (Hardwire) is LOW or HIGH, the behavior is the same.
Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_29 4.2.2 KL.15 (Hardwire) Status is LOW
 Not a Requirement Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_62 When KL.15 (Hardwire) is switched to LOW from HIGH, Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be OFF.
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_80 Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be OFF and "Passenger Airbag Indication Request” shall be set to “0” and sent to CAN message when KL.15 (Hardwire) is LOW.
 Supported R200 Open

SPS_SAIC_PAB_OFF_Lamp_63 When KL.15 (Hardwire) is switched to LOW from HIGH, Timer T1, T2 and T3 should reset.

Note 1: If KL.15 (Hardwire) is switched to LOW from HIGH during T1, no matter T1 is timeout or not, Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be OFF and reset T1;
Note 2: If KL.15 (Hardwire) is switched to LOW from HIGH during T2, no matter T2 is timeout or not, Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be OFF and reset T2.
Note 3: If KL.15 (Hardwire) is switched to LOW from HIGH during T3, no matter T3 is timeout or not, Passenger Airbag OFF Lamp should be OFF and reset T3.
 Supported R200 Open
